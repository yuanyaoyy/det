## Question
Talk about a celebrity that you respect. Who is this famous person? What aspects of this person and their work appeal to you?
## Answer

The primary reason I admire her is that she is not only an accomplished artist but also a person with profound social responsibility. In China, she is widely known for her powerful voice and emotionally moving songs. More importantly, she has devoted a significant portion of her income to charitable causes, especially helping orphans and people in need. She has even adopted several children and supported their education, which I find deeply inspiring.

Her actions had a direct impact on my personal values. In the past, I used to believe that helping others was not my responsibility and that it should be handled by the government instead. However, after listening to her songs and learning about her life story, my perspective changed completely. I began to take initiative in helping others in daily life.
